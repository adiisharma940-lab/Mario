01
Chapter One

THE ORDINARY
BOOKWORM

Ken Kaneki was a perfectly ordinary college student — quiet, bookish, a lover of literature with no greater ambition than a peaceful life. His world was books, coffee, and his best friend Hide. Then a date with a beautiful ghoul named Rize changed everything forever.

02
Chapter Two

BORN FROM
THE WRECKAGE

A near-fatal attack left Kaneki on the edge of death. To save him, surgeons transplanted Rize's ghoul organs into his body — creating the first half-human, half-ghoul hybrid. He woke up neither fully human nor fully ghoul, caught between two worlds, belonging to neither.

"I'M NOT THE PROTAGONIST OF A NOVEL OR ANYTHING. BUT IF YOU WROTE A STORY WITH ME AS THE LEAD — IT WOULD CERTAINLY BE A TRAGEDY."

— Ken Kaneki

03
Chapter Three

THE MASK
& THE MONSTER

To protect the people he loved, Kaneki donned a mask — the iconic black eyepatch crafted by Uta. Behind it he could be something else entirely. No longer the gentle bookworm. Something fiercer. The mask became a symbol of his duality, and the violence he was forced to embrace.

◎
Before the fall
HUMAN

Bookish. Gentle. Lonely. A student who found refuge in words, who loved without condition — and was destroyed for it.

◉
After the torture
GHOUL

White hair. Red eye. Boundless power born from suffering. He learned the weak have no rights — and chose to become strong enough to protect everyone.

04
Chapter Four

WHITE HAIR,
RED WORLD

Tortured for ten days by Jason of the Aogiri Tree, Kaneki's hair turned white — a physical manifestation of trauma that shattered and remade him. He emerged not broken but reborn, choosing to become the predator rather than the prey, the monster rather than the victim.

"THE WEAK HAVE NO RIGHTS OR CHOICES. THEIR ONLY FATE IS TO BE DEVOURED BY THE STRONG."

— Ken Kaneki

05
Chapter Five

THE PRICE
OF POWER

To grow stronger, Kaneki consumed other ghouls — a taboo act that warped both body and mind. His kagune mutated into a monstrous kakuja form, a grotesque armor born from cannibalism. Power always demands a price, and Kaneki paid in pieces of his humanity.
